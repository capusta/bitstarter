SuiMo -> (Suica x Passmo) + BitCoin

Got Bitcoins? Traveling to Japan? Hate carrying cash? We can help

Learn more »

get bitcoins

Visit bitstamp.com or mtgox.com to get some bitcoins up in your wallet. We will issue you a new BTC wallet which you can pre-charge with coins. Once you decide on the proper amount, let us know, and we'll convert it into Japanese e-cash cards.

we send you card

Yep, we ship you the card straight to your house before you leave. Yea we can probably use express mail - but c'mon, you should have already bought your plane tickets well in advance, so please plan accordingly and allow time for delivery.

use card

Use the cash card in over thousands of convenience stores, busses, train lines, station shops, etc ... there is not enough room on the website to list all the stores that take Suica or Passmo.

get remainder

Done using Suica or Passmo? Have a balance left over? Just send us the card back and we will refund you in Bitcoin whatever is left on the card. Including the deposit :)
